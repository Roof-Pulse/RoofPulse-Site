1. Overview

RoofPulse ("we," "us," or "our") operates getroofpulse.com and provides automated missed call recovery and lead follow-up services to roofing companies in the Memphis, TN area. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information from visitors to our website and from our clients.

Plain English summary: We collect only what we need to run our service. We don't sell your data. We don't share it with advertisers. We're a small local business and we treat your information the way we'd want ours treated.

2. Information We Collect

From website visitors:

  • Name, company name, phone number, and email address submitted through our contact form
  • Estimated missed call volume you provide voluntarily
  • Basic usage data such as pages visited and time on site (via standard server logs)

From clients (roofing companies we work with):

  • Business contact information and phone numbers
  • Call logs and text message records generated through our system
  • Lead and customer information that flows through the CRM we manage on your behalf
  • Appointment and booking data

We do not collect:

  • Payment card information — billing is handled by third-party processors
  • Social Security numbers or government ID
  • Sensitive personal information beyond what is needed to run the service

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries — when you fill out our contact form, we use your information to reach out personally
  • Deliver our service — setting up and maintaining your automated missed call system
  • Send you results and reports — weekly summaries and monthly performance data
  • Improve our service — understanding how the system performs helps us make it better
  • Communicate with you — updates, check-ins, and support

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating and improving RoofPulse.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We use GoHighLevel to power our automation platform, Formspree to process contact form submissions, and Resend for email delivery. These providers have their own privacy policies and are contractually required to protect your data.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or government authority, we may disclose information as legally required.
  • Business transfer: If RoofPulse is ever sold or transferred, your information may transfer as part of that transaction. We would notify you before that happens.
We will never sell your data. No advertisers, no data brokers, no third parties paying to reach you through us.

5. Client Business Data

When we manage your missed call system, we have access to call records, customer contact information, and conversation logs that belong to your business. Here is how we handle that:

  • Your data is yours. We are a custodian of your business data, not an owner. We do not use your customer data for any purpose other than operating your system.
  • Data export on cancellation. If you cancel, we will export all your leads, contacts, and conversation history and deliver it to you within 5 business days.
  • No cross-client sharing. Your customer data is never shared with or accessible to other RoofPulse clients.
  • Retention after cancellation. We delete your data from our systems within 30 days of cancellation unless you request otherwise.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses minimal tracking. We may use:

  • Essential cookies — required for the website to function properly
  • Analytics — basic page view data to understand how visitors use the site. We do not use Google Analytics or Facebook Pixel at this time.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Doing so will not affect your ability to use our website or contact us.

7. Data Retention

  • Contact form submissions: Retained for as long as the business relationship is active, or up to 2 years if no relationship develops
  • Active client data: Retained for the duration of the service agreement plus 30 days
  • Server logs: Retained for up to 90 days for security purposes

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your information — subject to any legal obligations we have to retain it
  • Opt out of any communications from us at any time
  • Export your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at Hayesscale500@gmail.com. We will respond within 5 business days.

9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information including:

  • HTTPS encryption on all web traffic
  • Access controls limiting who can view client data
  • Using reputable third-party platforms with their own security practices

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but we take it seriously and will notify you promptly if a breach affecting your data occurs.

10. Children's Privacy

RoofPulse is a business-to-business service. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 18. Our website and services are intended for business owners and professionals only.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. For active clients, we will notify you directly of any material changes. Continued use of our service after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
